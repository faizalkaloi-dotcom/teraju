Our Commitment to Confidentiality

Teraju IP Sdn Bhd recognises that every disclosure made by our clients — whether it concerns an invention, a business strategy, a product concept, or proprietary technical data — is commercially sensitive and of the highest personal value.

We are unequivocally committed to safeguarding your confidential information. All information received in the course of our engagement is treated with the utmost professional discretion, used solely for the purpose of providing intellectual property services, and protected against any unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse.
