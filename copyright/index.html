Why Certification Matters

Automatic Protection
Is Not Always Enough.

Under Malaysian law, copyright protection is granted automatically upon the creation of an original work. No registration, no filing, no official step required. Your painting, photograph, written work, or design is legally protected from the moment it exists.

But the law recognises a right that no one else can easily see. When a dispute arises — when someone copies your work or denies that it is yours — the burden falls on you to prove it. That is where a certificate changes everything.
