Utility Innovation

Local — MyIPO

A Malaysian IP right tailored for incremental improvements, simple inventions, and modifications of existing products or processes. Unlike a full patent, a Utility Innovation (UI) does not require a strict inventive step — making it faster, more affordable, and easier to obtain. Ideal for SMEs and first-time inventors seeking practical, enforceable protection without the complexity of full patent prosecution.

schedule Up to 20 years protection (subject to renewal)
verified No substantive inventiveness examination
precision_manufacturing

Full Patent

Local — MyIPO

The gold standard of IP protection in Malaysia. A full patent requires the invention to be new, involve an inventive step (non-obvious to a skilled person), and be industrially applicable. It undergoes substantive examination — a more rigorous process — but in return delivers higher legal credibility and stronger enforcement power. Exclusive rights to make, use, sell, or license the invention make it the right choice for technologically advanced or high-value innovations.

schedule 20 years protection (annual renewal)
gavel Substantive examination — highest credibility

PCT International

150+ Countries

Secure your invention across more than 150 member countries under the Patent Cooperation Treaty. A single PCT application preserves your priority date while giving you time to assess your target markets before incurring national phase costs.

language Global coverage, one filing
