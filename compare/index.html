Decision Framework

Which IP Is Right for You?

Different innovations call for different protections. Use this framework to identify the instrument that best safeguards your intellectual asset.

lightbulb

Patent

Invention

Grants exclusive rights to a novel, inventive, and industrially applicable creation — preventing others from manufacturing, using, or selling the invention without authorisation.

Protect this when you have:

check_circle

A new product, device, or machine

check_circle

A novel industrial process or method

check_circle

A unique technical solution to a problem

verified

Trademark

Brand Identity

Safeguards the distinctive signs that identify your business in the marketplace — ensuring consumers can reliably distinguish your goods and services from competitors.

Protect this when you have:

check_circle

A brand name or business name

check_circle

A tagline or slogan unique to your brand

check_circle

A logo, symbol, or distinctive mark

palette

Copyright

Creative Works

Protects original literary, artistic, and creative expressions — granting the author exclusive rights to reproduce, distribute, and adapt their work.

Protect this when you have:

check_circle

Original artwork, illustrations, or photography

check_circle

Written content, books, or scripts

check_circle

Music, software code, or film productions

category

Industrial Design

Aesthetic Design

Covers the visual and ornamental features of a product — protecting the appearance that makes an object aesthetically distinctive and commercially appealing.

Protect this when you have:

check_circle

A unique 3D product shape or form

check_circle

A distinctive 2D surface pattern or ornamentation

check_circle

Packaging or product configuration appealing to the eye
